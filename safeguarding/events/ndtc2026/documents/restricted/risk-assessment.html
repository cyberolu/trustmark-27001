Scope

This safeguarding risk assessment covers athletes, children, young people, parents, spectators, coaches, volunteers, officials, organisers and event personnel attending Nigeria Diaspora Sports Meet One.

The assessment is based on currently available event information and should be updated when attendance numbers, venue arrangements, staffing levels, medical provision or operational plans are confirmed.

Overall Risk Position

Based on current information, the overall safeguarding risk is assessed as medium until final attendance figures, venue arrangements, medical cover and volunteer numbers are confirmed.

The residual risk is expected to reduce once the safeguarding team register, event team register, volunteer briefing process and confirmed venue controls are completed.
